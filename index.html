Brief History

Started planning and construction late 2023 and began operations early 2024. Established by 14 doctors and professionals to offer accessible healthcare to the community.

Company Inspiration

The corporation aims to be the reliable center based healthcare facility which provides effective healthcare. A one stop shop healthcare facility that offers exceptional medical services from consultations, laboratories and other medical imaging services.
