PATIENT PRIVACY NOTICE

In compliance with RA 10173 also known as “Data Privacy Act of 2012”, this notice describes and defines patients' rights related to the use and disclosure of individual health information.

YOUR RIGHTS AS A PATIENT

  • Right to be informed of your health status
  • Right to ask for limits on the use and sharing of your health information
  • Right to ask for your health information to be sent to you in different ways
  • Right to view and get a copy of your health information
  • Right to change and correct your medical health history
  • Right to receive a record of when your health information was shared without your written consent
